## üöÄ Quick Start

### Step 1: Enter Property Details
- Policy number or name (optional)
- Inspection date (defaults to today)
- Service size (60, 100, or 200 Amps)
- Square footage (main floor required)

### Step 2: Check Equipment
Select which equipment is present:
- Heat pumps (specify quantity)
- Electric radiators or baseboards
- Other electric heating
- Electric water heater
- Electric range/stove
- Pool, hot tub, sauna, or vehicle charger
- Electric clothes dryer
- Electric pottery kiln

### Step 3: Get Results
Tap **"Calculate"** to see:
- Assessment summary
- Approximate load in amps
- Whether a safety survey is required, recommended, or not needed
- Detailed breakdown of all factors

### Step 4: Generate PDF
Tap **"Generate PDF"** to:
- Preview the report
- **On PC**: Click "Save" to download (check your downloads folder)
- **On Mobile**: Click "Save" to share/save via Android/iOS share menu

## üí° Tips

- **Accuracy Matters** - Double-check all inputs for the most accurate assessment
- **Multiple Heat Sources** - If you select multiple electric heating types, a safety survey will be recommended
- **Save Your Reports** - PDFs are named automatically with your policy info and date

## üìã Assessment Criteria

The app uses standard Canadian Electrical Code calculations:
- **200A Service**: Survey required >200A, recommended >170A
- **100A Service**: Survey required >100A, recommended >85A
- **60A Service**: Survey required >60A, recommended >50A
