Residential Electrical Service Checklist

For an accurate load test, photos of nameplates from all heating and major appliances must be provided. Ensure nameplate shows either AMPS or WATTS

Disclaimer: This checklist is intended for standard single-family dwellings only. It does not apply to multi-unit or commercial buildings. The appliances listed in Question 8 can significantly impact the calculated load. If more than one appliance from Question 8 is present, if you are uncertain about any part of this assessment or have questions regarding your specific situation, please contact a Safety Surveyor for further guidance.
